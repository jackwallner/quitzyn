No account, login, or personal information is required. All data (quit date, check-ins, journal, garden) is stored on-device in the app's App Group container. The app does not use non-exempt encryption.

GETTING STARTED
- On first launch, onboarding asks for the nicotine-free start date (any past date or today), optional daily spending and pouches-per-day, and a reminder hour, then a commitment step ("I commit to getting better" or "Not now") finishes setup.
- The app has 4 tabs: Home, Timeline, Health, Journal.
- Home: the day counter, the bonsai garden, and the "Check in for today" button. The top-left chart icon opens "Your progress" (milestones, savings, species). The top-right "..." menu has "Customize garden", "Settings", and "Reset counter".
- Timeline: calendar of nicotine-free days; selecting a day shows the tree as it looked then and lets you log or edit that day's check-in.
