Quit Zyn Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

Overview

Quit Zyn is a personal nicotine-free tracking app for iPhone and Apple Watch. The core experience is free. An optional Bloom+ subscription unlocks premium features. Your privacy is fundamental to how this app is built.

Data Collection

Quit Zyn does not collect, transmit, or share any of your personal data.

  • No usage data is sent to any server
  • No analytics or tracking of any kind
  • No third-party SDKs or frameworks (except RevenueCat for subscription management and Apple's StoreKit for purchases)
  • No advertising
  • No account creation required

Purchases & Subscriptions

Quit Zyn offers an optional Bloom+ subscription (and lifetime purchase) that unlocks premium features: full health benefit timeline with sources, journaling with daily prompts, all achievements, money, pouches, and nicotine saved statistics, and additional bonsai styles and garden decorations.

All payments are processed exclusively by Apple through StoreKit. Quit Zyn never receives, stores, or has access to your payment information, including credit card details, billing address, or Apple ID credentials.

To determine whether you have an active Bloom+ subscription or lifetime unlock, the app uses RevenueCat's SDK to verify receipt status. This check communicates with RevenueCat's servers solely for the purpose of confirming purchase status. RevenueCat does not receive any personal or health information, only anonymized purchase tokens.

Your purchase history and subscription management (upgrade, downgrade, cancel, or request a refund) are handled entirely by Apple through your Apple ID account. You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time in Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your device.

Following a free trial or paid subscription period, if you cancel, your access to Bloom+ features ends at the close of the current billing period. No data is lost. Your nicotine-free data and preferences remain on your device, and the free tier continues to work as before.

Local Storage

Quit Zyn stores all data locally on your device using SwiftData. This includes:

  • Nicotine-free journey history (start dates, reset reasons)
  • Daily check-ins (check-in status, mood, notes)
  • Journal entries
  • Garden state and species preferences
  • User settings (daily cost, pouches, reminder preferences)
  • Achievement and health benefit unlock progress

This data is stored in an App Group container (group.com.jackwallner.quitzyn) that is shared between the iOS app, widgets, and Apple Watch companion. This data never leaves your device.

Your preferences (goals, display settings, appearance) are stored locally in UserDefaults on your device.

Health Data

Quit Zyn does not read from or write to Apple HealthKit. All health-related information (daily check-ins, mood, health benefit tracking) is entered directly by you and stored locally. Quit Zyn does not access or process any HealthKit data types.

Data Export

Quit Zyn does not currently offer a data export feature. All data remains on your device within the app's local SwiftData store.

Data Retention & Deletion

All data remains on your device until you delete the app. Deleting Quit Zyn removes all locally stored data, including nicotine-free history, journal entries, and settings. No data persists on any external server because no data is ever transmitted externally.

To delete your data, uninstall the app from your device. No additional request is needed since no data exists outside your device.

Children's Privacy

Quit Zyn is not directed at children under the age of 13. The app does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.
